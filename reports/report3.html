Cybersecurity

David Lardner

What does it do?

What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the term used to describe the protection and security of IT systems all the way from the hardware to the software and data stored on them, from digital attacks. Cybersecurity not only covers the digital protection of these systems, but also the physical protection.
For example, a large business or data centre will have physical and digital security measures in place to prevent unauthorised access to IT systems such as servers, networking equipment or workstations. These security measures will depend on the size and nature of what is being protected. They can range from something as simple as using passwords and having equipment in a locked room, to something as complex as a secure fenced facility with 24hr surveillance, biometric locks, and data encryption.
Not only can a data breach be dangerous, but it can also be very expensive, costing on average $4.35 million USD globally in 2022. This is why cybersecurity is such an important aspect of the IT industry.

What is the state of the art of this new technology and what can be done now?
As we move more of our daily lives online and increase our reliance on the internet, cybercrime has grown and become a major threat. Cybercrime has been around since the dawn of the internet but its only in recent years that it has gained more attention from the general public. Some of the technology being used in cybersecurity today are things like artificial intelligence, behavioral analytics, and blockchain cybersecurity.

Artificial Intelligence
Artificial Intelligence, or AI, has been used in a variety of applications. In cybersecurity AI is being used to help IT security personnel identify potential vulnerabilities in their systems and to increase the speed at which they can be detected and stopped. This is done using AI technologies such as machine learning and natural language processing. Machine learning and natural language processing work together to help the AI analyse and learn from the data it collects or is given. By giving the AI data on previous cyber attacks it can identify patterns or irregularities that may indicate a cyber-attack. The AI can monitor and analyse the network in real time so it can learn the normal actions that take place. This way when something out of the ordinary occurs the AI can quickly and easily identify it and act before it becomes a threat.

Behavioural Anayltics
Behavioural analytics is a way of analysing the trends, patterns and behaviours of a user on a platform, such as a website or game (Wikipedia 2022). Behavioural analytics is used a lot in e-commerce, but it also can also be used in cybersecurity. By analysing the typical types of things a user does, a profile can be generated that can identify when something falls outside of the usual or expected behaviour. Behavioural analytics is usually used in conjunction with AI or other automated systems so that when such an event occurs, the system can act accordingly to prevent the threat. It could be as simple as prompting a user to verify they are not a robot using something like a CAPTCHA, or perhaps require further identification via two-factor authentication. It could also block access entirely.

Blockchain Cybersecurity
The blockchain is essentially a new type of information technology combining cryptography with distributed computing while being a completely decentralised system, meaning that it is not controlled by a single governing body or corporation. It works by having a network of computers work together to essentially create a shared and secure database. The database is built on a chain of blocks, each of which is an encrypted piece of data that is given a unique identifier called a hash. Before anything can be added to the chain, an algorithm is used to ensure all the computers on the network agree to the change so that no single computer can modify it. All the blocks are connected in chronological order and their hash value is dependant of the previous block. This means that a block cannot be deleted or modified without all subsequent blocks being changed as well. This method makes it nearly impossible to tamper with the data.

What technological or other developments make this possible?
Advancements in the cloud computing sector have helped improve cybersecurity. Having access to such a vast compute resource has allowed companies to develop more advanced AI systems and analyse large amounts of data much faster. The blockchain is often just associated with cryptocurrency, but as more attention is drawn to its security features, we will likely see it adopted into more data storage scenarios.

What is the potential impact of this development?

What is likely to change?
These technologies are going to be here to stay, the benefits they offer outweigh the risks. There are already laws regarding privacy, so we will likely these extended to include what data can be collected and how this data is managed in relation to cybersecurity.

Which people will be most affected and how?
These advancements in cybersecurity are going to benefit everyone. While big businesses are going to be affected greatly, it is everyday users of their services that will see it the most. For example, having a more secure banking service is going to provide not only piece of mind but also save customers money. The use of AI and behavioural analytics can help detect potential fraud activity and stop transactions before they cost people money. This is going to be of great benefit to people that are not as tech savvy when it comes to identifying fake websites and making purchases online.

Will this create, replace, or make redundant any current jobs or technologies?
The advancements in cybersecurity have the potential to both create new jobs and make other jobs obsolete. The use of AI will help in developing new systems and applications which may impact some roles. However, with the need for cybersecurity growing it will require more people, so we are likely to see more jobs being created than jobs lost.

How will this affect you?

In your daily life, how will this affect you?
These cybersecurity technologies would add additional security to our daily lives. While many of us are quite careful when it comes to online payments and internet security, it would not be hard for an accidental slip up to bring this all undone. By having our information stored securely using the blockchain, and using AI and behavioural analytics to identify when something out of the ordinary occurs we can minimise the impact a cybersecurity attack can have.

What will be different for you>
While nothing may change in how we do things from day to day, the processes that go on behind the scenes will help to ensure our daily lives are more secure. Having our banking institutions adopt more advanced cybersecurity technologies will ensure that our financial losses from cyber attacks are minimised. Governments and other organisations that have all sorts of personal information about us using more secure storage technologies will help prevent data theft. All of these things will benefit us and reduce our chance of becoming victim to cyber crime.

How might this affect members of your family or your friends?
Generally speaking, older family members are not great when it comes to technology and cybersecurity. It would be very easy for someone to catch them out with a phishing email or illegitimate website pretending to be one they would trust. The use of AI or behavioural analytics would help to protect them as it would identify the suspicious activity before they were able to do anything to compromise their security.
